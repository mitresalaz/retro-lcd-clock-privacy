Introduction

Retro LCD Clock ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our iOS application.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

Retro LCD Clock is designed with privacy as a top priority. The app:

  • Does NOT collect any personal information
  • Does NOT track your usage or behavior
  • Does NOT use analytics services
  • Does NOT contain advertising
  • Does NOT require an internet connection
  • Does NOT transmit any data to external servers
  • Does NOT use cookies or similar tracking technologies

Data Storage

The app stores only the following information locally on your device:

  • User Preferences: Your chosen theme, display settings (12/24 hour format, show/hide options), and visual effect preferences are stored using iOS UserDefaults
  • App State: Basic app state information required for the app to function

This data:

  • Remains entirely on your device
  • Is never transmitted to us or any third party
  • Is deleted when you delete the app
  • Is not backed up to iCloud
  • Cannot be accessed by us or anyone else

Permissions

Retro LCD Clock does not request any system permissions. The app:

  • Does NOT access your location
  • Does NOT access your contacts
  • Does NOT access your photos
  • Does NOT access your camera
  • Does NOT access your microphone
  • Does NOT access your calendar
  • Does NOT require notifications

The only system feature used is keeping the screen awake while the app is in the foreground, which is standard for clock applications.

Widgets

Home screen and lock screen widgets display the current time using your device's system clock. Widget data:

  • Is generated locally on your device
  • Uses only the current system time
  • Does not collect or transmit any information
  • Respects your app settings for theme and display format

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics services
  • Crash reporting services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud services

Children's Privacy

Retro LCD Clock does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages.
